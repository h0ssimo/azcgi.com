      Medieval Props

      May 07, 2021

      Get it on Unity Asset Store

      This medieval tavern props pack is perfect for your medieval, fantasy project! This set is mainly suitable for building a medieval tavern, pubs or houses and it can be easily used everywhere! It's usable for video games, archViz, cinematic's, etc. There is 1 overview level with dynamic lights showcasing the 70 ready to use prefabs.

      This pack features a Master shader with 7 parameters, which offers you the ability to easily change effects or color corrections on Materials to perfectly fit your vision. It's also using texture atlas, this way you can create prefabs and merge actors that will use a very few material slots to reduce drastically draw calls.
